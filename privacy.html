Hurofi — Learn Arabic for Kids

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
Developer: Digitovex (Alaa Alsarayrah), Dubai, United Arab Emirates

The short version for parents

Hurofi is built so that we never see your child's data. The app works fully offline. We do not collect, transmit, store on our servers, or sell any personal information. There are no ads, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party advertising SDKs. Everything your child does in Hurofi stays on your device.

What information the app stores — and where

During setup, a parent may enter a child's nickname, choose an age range (3–5 or 6–8), and pick a companion character. The app also saves learning progress (completed levels and stars), sound preferences, and a record that a parent accepted these terms.

All of this is stored only on your device, in the app's local storage. It is never transmitted to Digitovex or anyone else. Deleting the app, or using "Reset progress" in Settings, permanently erases it. We have no copy and no way to recover it — by design.

What we do NOT do

  • No analytics or telemetry SDKs of any kind
  • No advertising and no ad networks
  • No account creation — we never ask for an email, phone number, or password
  • No camera, microphone, contacts, or location access
  • No collection of names, photos, or any personal information on our servers
  • No sale or sharing of data — we have nothing to sell or share

Purchases (the one third party involved)

Hurofi offers optional premium subscriptions and a one-time purchase, available only behind a parental gate. Payments are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play under their own terms and privacy policies — we never see your payment details.

To check whether a purchase is active, the app uses RevenueCat, a subscription-management service. RevenueCat receives an anonymous random identifier and purchase receipt information from the app store. It does not receive your child's nickname, age, or any profile information, and we do not link purchase data to any personal information (we hold none). RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

This purchase check is the only network activity in the app, and it only occurs in connection with the parent-gated purchase and restore features. All learning content works fully offline.

Text-to-speech

Arabic pronunciation uses the text-to-speech engine built into your device's operating system (Apple iOS / Google Android). Speech is generated on the device. If a parent enters a nickname, the app may speak it locally as part of the greeting; the nickname never leaves the device.

Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K and similar laws)

Hurofi is designed for children aged 3–8 and is built to the principle of data minimization: the best way to protect children's data is not to collect it. Because no personal information is collected or transmitted by Digitovex, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on any server. Parents control all on-device data and can erase it at any time via Settings → Reset progress, or by deleting the app.

Setup steps that involve any choice about the child (nickname, consent, purchases) sit behind a parental gate intended for adults.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change what the app does with data — for example, adding optional analytics in a future version — we will update this policy first, change the "Last updated" date, and present the change to parents in the app before it takes effect.
